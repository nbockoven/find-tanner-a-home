Background

Tanner has been part of our family since he was 8 weeks old. He's accustomed to a child-free home. It's just been my wife, our pomeranian, and myself living with him.

He's been in good health all these years. He's never had any health issues, and is up-to-date on vaccinations. He is missing two of his small, front teeth.

Likes

He likes cuddling on the couch, sunbathing outside or in a sunspot, going for walks, and sticking his head out the window while cruising in the car. Like most Shiba Inus, he's loyal, clean, smart, and athletic. He sits and comes on command. If you're in need of a "high five", he's willing to give you one. He thrives on attention, but also likes his alone time. He'll find a spot of his own; prefers a corner to relax by himself.

Temperament

Besides being wary of strangers and noises outside, he's a chill dog. He's usually relaxing in the house or outside. He enjoys being active by playing and having adventures that stimulate his mind. He's a very smart dog; learns quickly. Doesn't like to be continually pestered by young children.
